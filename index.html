Welcome to The Professional Vehicle Technicians Network

Our goal is to provide an environment that fosters education and growth through networking with like-minded technicians in a non-competitive environment, because at the end of the day we are colleagues and friends, all facing similar problems.

Members of thePVTN adhere to the highest professional standards of automotive vehicle repair.

First and foremost we are honest with ourselves, each other and our customers. We want to do the job right and at a fair price while abiding to applicable laws and regulations.

Second, we continue to learn. From training sessions, schools, and just as importantly, each other. We all have different experiences, specialties, methods, and techniques, and we enjoy sharing them with each other.

Third, we are respectful. We understand the differences in our training and experiences and know that while some of us may be very experienced or even retired we can still learn from someone just starting out. For those of you just starting out - respect those of us who have come before you but don't be afraid to ask questions or share what you know.

Register to take part in the discussions, banter and camaraderie.
